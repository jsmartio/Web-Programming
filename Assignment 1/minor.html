Minor in Computer Science

A minor in computer science is an excellent complement to a degree in Liberal Arts or Business Administration. Students in the sciences and mathematics may also find the minor program attractive. The computer science department offers a selection of courses covering programming and software development, design and analysis of computer systems, computer architecture, computer theory, and computer applications.
