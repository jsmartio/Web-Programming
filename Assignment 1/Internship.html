-Internships-

Internships are integral for preparing students for post-graduate opportunities. You’ll gain valuable experience, build a professional network and learn more about yourself and potential careers.

Interns have the opportunity to:

  • Get a better grasp of the connections between theory and practice
  • Adapt to a culture outside the college environment
  • Network with professionals at different levels of an organization
  • Apply academic knowledge to hands-on situations
  • Gain exposure to potential career choices
  • Earn academic credit

And don’t forget: on average, employers extended job offers to 51% of their 2014 interns according to a 2014 NACE student survey.
